# portfolio-comms
HTML AR Monthly Communications Tool

Sending personalized collection emails to hundreds of accounts every month is time-consuming and repetitive. 
This tool doesn't automate the entire process — but it eliminates most of the manual work.

Load your AR export as a CSV, and the tool instantly reads your portfolio, assigns the right email template to each account based on its collection status, and generates a ready-to-send message with a professionally formatted balance table — open AR, past due, over 60 days, and more — all in one click.

34 collection statuses covered. Accounts that need a manual action instead of an email (disputes, bankruptcy, write-offs) show a visual alert so nothing gets missed. Every template is editable, and changes save permanently into the tool itself.

In Step 4, you open your Email , hit Compose/Create, and copy-paste each field — recipient, CC, subject (name of the account with Month & year), and body — directly from the tool. The formatted table pastes into your mail with full formatting intact.

No subscriptions. No integrations, No APIs, No data leaves your browser. Just a faster, more organized way to get your monthly communications done.
